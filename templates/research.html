Study Details: AI-Assisted Rehab

Objectives

To evaluate the efficacy of AI-driven gait analysis and robotic exoskeletons in improving mobility for post-stroke patients over a 24-month period.

Requirements

  • Participate in bi-weekly assessment sessions.
  • Willingness to use wearable sensor technology.
  • No conflicting major surgeries planned in next 6 months.
