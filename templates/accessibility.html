Facility Accessibility

City General Hospital's campus is designed with universal access in mind. We've removed physical barriers to ensure all patients can navigate our facility with dignity.

  • Wheelchair Navigation: All major pathways are minimum 60 inches wide to allow two wheelchairs to pass easily.
  • Auditory Support: Every elevator and public consultation room is equipped with hearing loop technology.
  • Visual Aids: Braille signage is located at the entrance to every department and room throughout the hospital.

Language Services

Language should never be a barrier to care. We provide comprehensive translation and interpretation services:

  • Professional medical interpreters available 24/7 in over 40 languages.
  • Translation of all vital medical documents, including consent forms and discharge instructions.
  • ASL (American Sign Language) interpretation services for the deaf and hard of hearing.
