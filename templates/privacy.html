Section 01

Introduction

At City General Hospital, your trust is our most valuable asset. We are dedicated to maintaining the confidentiality of your personal and medical information in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and all applicable state and federal laws.

This policy outlines how we handle your information across all our digital platforms, including our AI navigation system (HospNav AI) and our integrated Patient Portal.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with the best possible medical care and navigation experience:

  • Personal Identity: Name, date of birth, contact information (email, phone), and government ID.
  • Health Information: Medical history, current diagnoses, treatment plans, and imaging records.
  • Navigation Data: Aggregated, anonymized paths within the hospital to optimize HospNav AI routes.
  • Billing Data: Insurance provider details, policy numbers, and payment transaction history.
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

Your data enables us to provide high-quality care and innovative navigation services:

  • Care Coordination: Sharing medical records between your primary care physician and specialists within our network.
  • AI Optimization: Using anonymized navigation data to predict hallway congestion and suggest the fastest routes for patients.
  • Portal Integration: Syncing your appointments and prescriptions automatically to your secure Patient Portal.
  • Communications: Sending automated appointment reminders, health alerts, and emergency notifications.
Section 04

Data Security

We employ military-grade security protocols to ensure your data remains protected from unauthorized access:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All medical data is encrypted using AES-256 both at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3).
  • Biometric Access: Our internal systems require multi-factor authentication and, in clinical areas, biometric verification.
  • Security Audits: Continuous real-time monitoring and weekly third-party vulnerability assessments.
Section 05

Your Rights

You have full control over your health information. Under HIPAA, you are entitled to:

  • Access and download your complete medical records via the Patient Portal.
  • Request amendments to any clinical information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities (excluding those required for treatment).
  • Receive a full disclosure report of who has accessed your records.
Section 06

Data Sharing

City General Hospital does not sell your data. We only share information with:

  • Treatment teams directly involved in your clinical care.
  • Insurance providers for claims processing and payment verification.
  • Regulatory bodies as required by law (e.g. public health reporting).
